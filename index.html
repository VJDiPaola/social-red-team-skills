How to use the skills

Save the two .md files into your Claude skills folder, or attach them inline in a project. The frontmatter tells Claude when to auto-trigger.

Then paste a starter prompt from the handout. Sparring partner goes first if the idea is still mostly enthusiasm. Pre-mortem goes next, before any money or hires.
